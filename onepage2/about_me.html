ABOUT ME

You guessed it right!
I want to give myself a fresh start as a web developer and this website is my portfolio. I have created it to demonstrate my knowledge and skills.

For many years I was a successful manager and was engaged in computer technology from the managerial point of view. My life changed drastically when our family has moved to a new country.

This change was not only a challenge but also an opportunity . I was not afraid to start all over again and got the chance to embody myself where I am strongest.

I have always been distinguished by independent thinking, the ability to analyze information and build logical systems, and an impeccable business reputation.

Why do I want to become a web developer? There are two reasons.

Firstly, I get aesthetic satisfaction when I see a good website. It gives me pleasure to think, find an elegant solution, write code and see its visual interpretation.

Secondly, having many years of experience in business, I clearly understand my strengths and weaknesses.My intuition tells me that a web developer profession is mine. Here I will be both useful and successful.

If it requires a lot of work and study - I'm ready! Now I finish my studies at Wilmington University with a degree in computer science and work as a Walt Disney World's bus driver .

In addition, I'm gaining practical knowledge by taking the "Front-End Engineer" course at Codecademy. I am sure that I will achieve the goal, and the golden key to it is my motivation and diligence.

My studies and work leave almost no free time. Nevertheless, I travel with my family and companions - my Subaru and Nikon when I can. All photos on this website are mine.
